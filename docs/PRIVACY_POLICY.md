# Privacy Policy for AisyFit

**Last Updated:** January 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **AisyFit** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered fitness coaching application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with you ("Personal Information").

### A. Information You Provide to Us

- **Account Registration:** Name, email address, password, and profile image.
- **Health and Fitness Data:** Gender, age, weight, height, activity level, fitness goals, dietary preferences, allergies, injuries, and workout history.
- **User Content:** Feedback, logs, check-ins, and any photos you upload (e.g., progress photos).

### B. Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, Android ID), and IP address.
- **Usage Data:** Details of your visits to our application, traffic data, logs, and other communication data and the resources that you access and use.
- **Metrics:** Daily step counts, water intake, and other tracked metrics if functionality is enabled and permissions granted.

### C. AI Processing

- We use Artificial Intelligence (AI) to generate personalized workout and diet plans. The data you provide (goals, injuries, preferences) is processed by our AI algorithms to create these plans.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Provide and Manage the Service:** To create personalized account profiles, generate AI-driven workout and diet plans, and track your progress.
- **Improve the Service:** To analyze usage patterns, train our AI models (on anonymized/aggregated data), and fix bugs.
- **Communication:** To send you updates, security alerts, and support messages.
- **Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

## 4. Specific Provisions for Health Data

**AisyFit** treats your health and fitness data with high sensitivity.

- We do **not** sell your personal health data to third parties.
- We use your health data solely to provide the fitness and nutrition coaching features of the app.
- If you integrate with Apple HealthKit or Google Fit, we use that data only to update your progress within the app and do not use it for advertising purposes.

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We may share information with:

- **Service Providers:** Third-party vendors who perform services for us, such as:
  - **Supabase:** For secure database hosting and authentication.
  - **AI Providers:** To process data and generate content (though data is typically anonymized where possible).
  - **Analytics Providers:** To help us understand how the app is used.
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights.
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger, sale, or asset transfer.

## 6. Data Security and Retention

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information.

- **Security:** We utilize industry-standard encryption and secure infrastructure (Supabase) to store your data.
- **Retention:** We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or to comply with legal obligations.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access and Correction:** You can access and edit your profile information directly within the app.
- **Account Deletion:** You can request the deletion of your account and associated data directly through the application settings or by contacting us. Upon deletion request, we will permanently remove your personal data from our active databases.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 9. Third-Party Links

The Application may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third parties.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
